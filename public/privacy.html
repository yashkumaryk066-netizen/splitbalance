Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 03, 2026

SettleStack (also referred to as "SplitBalance") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, create groups, or upload expense data. This includes your name, email address, and transaction details.

2. Use of Information

We use the information to synchronize your expenses across devices, notify group members of updates, and provide the core functionality of the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Infrastructure). We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Camera and Contacts

The app requests camera access to allow you to scan and upload receipts. It requests contact access to help you invite friends to your groups easily.

5. Your Choices

You can delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us or through the app settings.
